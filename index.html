WHAT IS THIS?

Comedy Hack Day is a project of Cultivated Wit.

Comedy Hack Day is an overnight hackathon that brings together comedians and developers. We held the first one in NYC last September. Read Fast Company's review here. If you're interested in sponsoring the event or just want to know more, email us.

Check out the Hacker League page from our NYC edition or the trailer below to see what's made during Comedy Hack Day.
