Wait, what is this?

Comedy Hack Day is an overnight hackathon during which comedians, designers, and developers work together to build the most hilarious apps and hardware hacks. The participants pitch their ideas on Saturday morning, then team up and spend the rest of the weekend building their projects. At the end, all the teams demo their projects in what’s pretty much the most nerdy and awesome comedy show we've ever witnessed.

Our last Comedy Hack Day was held at Twitter HQ in April. Check out the highlight reel to see why we think Comedy Hack Day is the best hackathon ever.
